# Design System

## Theme & Style
The aesthetic is "Tactical Modernism." It combines the rugged, high-stakes atmosphere of defense with the sleek, polished finish of a premium technology firm. The site uses a high-contrast layout, alternating between stark white light sections and immersive "Midnight" dark sections. Layouts feature sharp 0px border-radii to emphasize precision and rigidity.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Midnight):** HSL(220, 15%, 5%) - A deep, near-black blue-tinted neutral.
- **Secondary (Pure White):** HSL(0, 0%, 100%) - Used for high-impact typography and clean sections.
- **Accent (Tactical Teal):** HSL(151, 72%, 57%) - A vibrant, glowing green-teal used for links, active states, and HUD elements.
- **Overlays (Glass):** Black/60% with backdrop-blur-xl for technical readability.

## Typography
- **Headings:** Montserrat (ExtraBold/700) for H1/H2. Uppercase for a commanding presence.
- **Sub-headings:** Raleway (SemiBold/600) for H3/H4.
- **Body Text:** Arial for technical data and Times New Roman (Italic) for mission-critical/legacy statements. Base size: 14px for readability, 12px for technical metadata.

## Layout & Spacing
- **Base Unit:** 12px.
- **Container:** 1440px max-width for content, while background elements bleed to 100% width.
- **Vertical Rhythm:** Large `py-32` (128px) padding between major sections to create breathing room and emphasize importance.

## Components
- **Buttons:** Sharp 0px corners. Solid teal with black text for primary; outlined white/teal for secondary. Hover state includes a subtle offset border treatment.
- **Cards:** No shadows. Border-less with dark overlays. Imagery is the focus.
- **Inputs:** Underlined or minimally bordered, high-contrast focus states.

## Imagery & Icons
- **Style:** Cinematic, high-shutter-speed tactical photography. Desaturated colors or high-contrast monochrome treatments.
- **Icons:** Thin-stroke technical icons in tactical teal.

## Motion & Animation
- **Transitions:** Smooth, linear-out-slow-in timing around 400ms.
- **Hover:** Slight scale-up for cards with a teal color-tint shift.
- **Scroll:** Sections reveal with vertical slide-up or shutter-style motion.
